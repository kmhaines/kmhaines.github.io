I am a first year MLIS student. In fact, this is my second quarter of my degree!

Where am I?

Goshen County, Wyoming - the south-eastern corner of the state.

Why Library and Information Science?

A few reasons. First, I have degrees in music and history, both with focus on medieval history and music. This may seem like an odd tangent, but, in fact, the study of history lends itself well to library science. A study of history inherently lends itself to the ideas of preservation of knowledge and access to knowledge, which then leads to libraries, as they have been preservers of knowledge throughout history and are important for access of information.
Secondly, I started working in the Goshen County Library in September of 2023 and in doing so, I discovered that I loved it and I did, in fact, want to continue in library work in my future.

Academic Interests

  1. My current library science interests are in web content (hence taking this class) and community outreach to disenfranchised groups, specifically LGBTQ+ community members.
  2. I am also interested in library history and historical developments. Last quarter, I wrote a paper looking at newspaper articles around the founding of my public library. There was quite the drama over the library board appointments in 1922!
